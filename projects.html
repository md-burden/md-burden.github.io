Recipeasy

Recipeasy is intended to be a one-stop-shop for professional and amateur chefs alike to share their creations. Whether you have been cooking for 50 years or 5 months there is a place at Recipeasy for your creations. Users will be able to view and save recipes as well as leave a comment for the creator to respond to.
